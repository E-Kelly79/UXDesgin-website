About Tramore

The seaside town of Tramore, only 13km from Waterford City, is one of Ireland most visited and loved towns. Aptly named, as it is derived from the Irish 'Trá Mhor' which translates as 'Big Strand', the town itself is built on hills rising up from the beautiful beach, which gives fantastic views of the Atlantic Ocean.

A popular coastal resort, Tramore’s golden sandy beach is one of Ireland’s longest beaches at 5km. The rolling Atlantic waves attract both swimmers and surfers, with surf lessons and equipment for hire available locally. Sailing and sea kayaking are also available seasonally. Tramore is also renowned for its beach angling, with cliffs along the coast offering excellent rock and reef fishing.
